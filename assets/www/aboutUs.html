About App

Anchoring is a self-management technique that is proven to help people that suffer from panic attacks.

This technique is designed to add positive associations to events that trigger an attack.

Anchoring involves three steps:

Step 1 - Visualization

Think of a time and place that you felt most happy.

Hold it big and clear in your mind.

Step 2 - Anchor it with a gesture

With an image clear in your mind add a gesture to anchor it.

This could be a touch or motion such as rubbing the back of your hand or touching your thumb and index finger.

Step 3 - Reinforce it with a tell

To help embed the anchor, repeat a short tell and repeat it as you visualise and gesture.

A tell could be a couple of words or a short phrase that helps to reinforce the new anchor.

When used together, these three steps form a powerful tool to help people overcome times of high anxiety and panic.

How can this app help?

Anxious Away has been devloped to help you record, track and share your thoughts and experiences with others using the anchoring technique.

We hope that by recording this information you can start to learn more about your condition and the mechanisms that have been proven to work for you.
